Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Food Recognition System?

The Food Recognition System is an AI-based application that identifies food items from images using advanced machine learning algorithms.

What technology powers the Food Recognition System?

Our system is built using state-of-the-art machine learning frameworks and custom algorithms designed specifically for food recognition tasks.

How do I use the system?

Simply upload an image of the food item you wish to identify, and the system will analyze the image and provide you with the name and information about the food.

Is the Food Recognition System free to use?

Yes, our basic food identification service is free for personal use.
